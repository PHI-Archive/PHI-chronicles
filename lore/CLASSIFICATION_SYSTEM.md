# Classification System

---

## Chronicle Categories

### Location Interaction

A recurring paranatural interaction associated with a specific place.

Example:

JHI-003

---

### Entity Interaction

An interaction involving an intelligent or semi-intelligent non-human presence.

---

### Object Interaction

An interaction associated with a physical object.

---

### Event Interaction

A recurring or spontaneous phenomenon.

---

### Risk Levels

Level| Description
Negligible| No known danger
Low| Minor psychological or physical effects
Moderate| Potential injury or prolonged effects
High| Severe injury or fatality possible
Critical| Community-level threat

---

### Reliability Index

Index| Description
A| Directly verified
B| Multiple corroborating sources
C| Limited evidence
D| Unverified
X| Unknown

---

### Record Types

Suffix| Description
INT| Interview
I| Incident
PR| Police Record
CR| Court Record
PH| Photograph
AUD| Audio Record
ADD| Addendum

Examples:

JHI-003-INT01

JHI-003-I01

JHI-003-PR01
