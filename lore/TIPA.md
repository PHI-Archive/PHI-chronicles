# Threshold Initiative for Paranatural Affairs

## Designation

TIPA

## Common Name

Threshold

---

## Overview

The Threshold Initiative for Paranatural Affairs is an investigative organization responsible for documenting and responding to reported paranatural interactions.

TIPA maintains operational neutrality regarding the origin of documented phenomena.

The Initiative does not classify interactions as supernatural, psychological, or scientific unless sufficient evidence exists to support such conclusions.

---

## Mission Statement

The purpose of TIPA is:

- Documentation
- Investigation
- Preservation of evidence
- Public safety

---

## Archive Policy

Witness statements shall be archived in their original language whenever possible.

Translations are considered supplementary records.

---

## Motto

"Cross the threshold. Record what remains."
