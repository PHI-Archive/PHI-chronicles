# PHI-005-PR01

### Type: Police Record
### Source: Local Police Inspection Log
### Date: 15 February 2024
### Location: Central Java, Indonesia

---

## Summary

Local authorities were contacted after repeated complaints regarding a light observed inside an abandoned rural structure after dark.

Responding officers found no occupants inside the building. No active electrical source was present. No candles, lanterns, or batteries were recovered during inspection.

The house showed signs of long-term neglect, including damaged roofing, weathered woodwork, and collapsed interior furnishings. No footprints, fresh disturbances, or evidence of recent habitation were documented.

A search of the surrounding area produced no intruders and no physical explanation for the reported illumination.

Officers noted that the structure was visible from a road where a streetlamp had been installed in recent years. However, residents insisted that reports of the light existed before the lamp was placed.

The matter was forwarded to TIPA for review.

---

## Officer Remarks

One officer wrote that the building “looked empty, but not comfortable to stand near.”

Another noted that the area felt unusually quiet during inspection, though no objective anomaly was recorded.

No paranatural evidence was recovered.

---

## Disposition

Case referred to TIPA field personnel.
